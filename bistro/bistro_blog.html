Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2012

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique cakked sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into water bath taht is precisely set to the target temperature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Geeks Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
